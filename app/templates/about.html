Hi, I'm Remy Davison.

I'm a junior communications design major at Carnegie Mellon University with a particular interest in advertising. While new media and technology are exciting to me, I also have an interest in more traditional forms of communications design, like bookmaking and general print media.

I'm also minoring in creative writing. Once, I even got published in Chicken Soup for the Soul. I'm also interested in comedy and theatre. I believe having passions and interests outside of design benefits me when I am working. For me, design is so distinctly human that you can't separate it totally from the designer, which is why I strive to check my own biases and get many outside perspectives. I value collaboration, honesty, creativity, and kindness.
